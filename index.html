004 / APPROACH — WHO I AM

M-SHAPED

I don't pick a lane.
I work in the overlap.

Most AV managers can't write software. Most developers have never tuned a room. I do both — and the interesting work lives in the space between.

By day I engineer responsive environments: sensing platforms, reactive projection, and live audio infrastructure across dozens of spaces. The web work comes from the same instinct — make the thing aware, make it respond, make it feel alive.

That combination is the whole point. Anyone can bolt on an animation. I build systems that sense and react, because that's what I do in the physical world every day.
